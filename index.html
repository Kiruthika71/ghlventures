Why property flipping

A wealth creation strategy involves acquiring a stressed property or undervalued property at a relatively lower price, often much lower than the market value, and then making improvements/ developments to increase its market appeal and value to sell at a higher price, ideally within a short period.

High Profit Potential

A Property flipped with proper market knowledge, strategic planning, effective disposal, and risk management strategies can indeed provide a high profit potential than any other kind of real estate investment

Tangible Asset

Unlike stocks or bonds, the investment is backed by physical ownership of the property which is appealing for investors who prefer the security of a real estate holding

Quick Turnaround

Compared to holding real estate for rental income, flipping offers a quicker way to see a return on your investment.

Short-Term Holding Period

Property flipping typically involves a comparatively short holding period, often ranging from a few months to less than a year, which is contrasts to the common practice in long-term real estate investment of buy-and-hold strategy.
